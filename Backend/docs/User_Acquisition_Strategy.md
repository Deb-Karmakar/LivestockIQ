# User Acquisition Strategy - LivestockIQ

> Go-to-market strategy for acquiring users from zero awareness to active adoption.

## Executive Summary

LivestockIQ employs a **Vet-First, Government-Backed** user acquisition strategy. By leveraging the trust chain between veterinarians and farmers, and the regulatory authority of DoAH&D, we achieve sustainable adoption aligned with ground realities of Indian livestock management.

> **One-Liner:** *"We don't chase farmers. We empower veterinarians — and they bring the farmers to us."*

---

## Understanding Target Segments

| Segment | Profile | Pain Point | Motivation |
|---------|---------|------------|------------|
| **Farmers** | Rural, low-tech literacy, Hindi/regional language | Fear of drug residue penalties, animal health issues | Better prices for compliant products |
| **Veterinarians** | Semi-urban, tech-savvy, regulated profession | Paper-based records, no treatment tracking | Professional credibility, license compliance |
| **Regulators** | Government officials, DoAH&D | No real-time visibility, manual audits | Data for policy decisions, AMR reduction |

## Phase 1: Government-Led Adoption (Top-Down)

### Leverage SIH & Ministry Connection

Since LivestockIQ addresses **SIH Problem Statement 25007** from the Department of Animal Husbandry & Dairying, leverage government channels:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry & Dairying (DoAH&D)   │
│                         ↓                                       │
│  State Animal Husbandry Departments                            │
│                         ↓                                       │
│  District Veterinary Hospitals / Block Offices                  │
│                         ↓                                       │
│  Registered Veterinarians → Farmers under their supervision     │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Action Items

| Action | Timeline | Owner |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Pilot with 1-2 high-livestock districts (UP, Rajasthan, Gujarat, MP) | Month 1-3 | Business Dev |
| Integrate with existing schemes (Pashudhan Sanjivani, NADRS, e-GOPALA) | Month 3-6 | Product Team |
| Mandate registration for vets receiving government vaccines/medicines | Month 6+ | Ministry Partnership |
| Distribute IEC materials at veterinary hospitals | Ongoing | Marketing |

### Target Districts for Pilot

| State | District | Livestock Population | Why |
|-------|----------|---------------------|-----|
| Uttar Pradesh | Bareilly | High cattle density | Major dairy belt |
| Rajasthan | Bikaner | High camel/cattle | Arid zone livestock |
| Gujarat | Anand | Amul cooperative base | Existing digital infrastructure |
| Madhya Pradesh | Indore | Mixed livestock | Progressive farmers |

## Phase 2: Veterinarian-First Approach (Trust Chain)

### Why Vets First?

- Each vet supervises **50-200+ farmers**
- Farmers **trust their local vet** more than any government app
- Vets are the **gatekeepers** to antimicrobial usage

### Vet Acquisition Channels

| Channel | Strategy | Expected Conversion |
|---------|----------|---------------------|
| **State Veterinary Councils** | Offer CPD credits for app usage | 30% |
| **Veterinary Colleges** | Campus presentations to BVSc final years | 20% |
| **WhatsApp/Telegram Groups** | Share success stories, demo videos | 15% |
| **Vet Conferences & Expos** | Live demos of blockchain prescription signing | 25% |
| **IVRI Alumni Networks** | Peer recommendations | 10% |

### Vet Value Proposition

```
┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  "Start your digital practice with LivestockIQ"                │
├────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  ✓ Digital prescriptions with RSA signatures                  │

│  ✓ Professional credibility & regulatory alignment            │
│  ✓ Unique vetId to onboard your farmers                       │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Incentives for Vets

## Phase 3: Farmer Onboarding (Through Vets)

### Vet-to-Farmer Onboarding Flow

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  1. Vet registers on LivestockIQ                             │
│                    ↓                                          │
│  2. Vet receives unique vetId (e.g., "RAJ2024VET007")        │
│                    ↓                                          │
│  3. During farm visits, vet registers farmer on mobile app   │
│     └── Uses farmer's phone OR vet's phone                    │
│     └── Speech-to-text for illiterate farmers                │
│     └── Scans animal ear tags on the spot                    │
│                    ↓                                          │
│  4. Farmer gets SMS with login credentials                   │
│     "आपका LivestockIQ अकाउंट बन गया है। App डाउनलोड करें:"   │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### On-Ground Activation Channels

| Channel | Why It Works | Action |
|---------|--------------|--------|
| **Milk Collection Centers** | Farmers visit daily; trusted touchpoint | Partner with dairy cooperatives (Amul, NDDB) |
| **Animal Markets/Haats** | Weekly congregation of livestock traders | Demo booths, instant registrations |
| **Krishi Vigyan Kendras** | Extension services, training programs | Train progressive farmers as ambassadors |
| **Self-Help Groups (SHGs)** | Women livestock keepers (poultry, goats) | Target through NRLM networks |
| **Common Service Centers** | Village-level digital infrastructure | Use CSC operators as registration agents |

### Farmer Value Proposition

```
┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  "जानें कब आपका दूध बेचने के लिए सुरक्षित है"                   │
│  (Know when your milk is safe to sell)                         │
├────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  ✓ Withdrawal period alerts before sale                       │
│  ✓ Treatment records connected to your vet                    │
│  ✓ AI health tips in Hindi                                    │
│  ✓ Premium prices for compliant products                      │
│  ✓ Works offline in low-connectivity areas                    │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Incentives for Farmers

| Action | Reward |
|--------|--------|
| Register first animal | ₹50 mobile recharge OR 1 month free AI tips |
| Complete 10 treatments with vet | "Trusted Farmer" badge |
| Maintain 100% MRL compliance for 3 months | Certificate + priority in govt schemes |
| Refer 3 farmers who register | Free health check for 1 animal |

## Phase 5: Regulator Adoption

### Strategy

Regulators adopt **AFTER** farmers and vets are active — they need data to monitor.

| Step | Action | Outcome |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Demo to DoAH&D leadership with anonymized pilot data | Buy-in |
| 2 | Show ROI: "Reduce field inspections by 40%" | Budget approval |
| 3 | Integrate with NADCP | Official mandate |
| 4 | Mandatory reporting for subsidized farms | Universal adoption |

### Regulator Value Proposition

```
┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  "Real-time visibility into antimicrobial usage nationwide"   │
```

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Low smartphone penetration | Vet-assisted registration, feature phone SMS alerts |
| Internet connectivity | Offline-first mobile app design |
| Language barriers | Hindi/regional language support, voice input |
| Trust issues | Government endorsement, local vet involvement |
| Competition from existing apps | Unique blockchain + AI features, regulatory alignment |
